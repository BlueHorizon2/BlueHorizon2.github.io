ABOUT

My name is Seamus, I am 20 years old with an interest in photography. I take a variety of photos, including action, landscape, and wildlife. I do all my editing myself in Adobe Lightroom and Topaz DeNoise. I currently use a Canon EOS 450D with 18-55mm, 55-250mm, and 75-300mm lenses. I also do some mobile photography on my Poco X3 Pro. I am constantly learning new techniques for getting the best possible image out of a situation.
